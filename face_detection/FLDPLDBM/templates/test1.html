    Advanced Technologies Integrated

    Federated Learning

    Ensuring data privacy by enabling decentralized model training across multiple devices without sharing sensitive user data.

    Differential Privacy

    Protecting user identity by injecting controlled noise into data, ensuring that individual records remain untraceable while maintaining data utility.

    Fully Homomorphic Encryption

    Allowing computations on encrypted data without decryption, ensuring end-to-end security in facial recognition processes.

    Liveness Detection

    Preventing spoof attacks by distinguishing between real users and fake representations, such as photos, videos, or 3D masks.
